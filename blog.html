How I Analyze Data

Data analysis is about storytelling with numbers. I begin by understanding the problem, cleaning the dataset, and then using Python and SQL to explore the data. Visualization tools like Power BI or Tableau help me bring insights to life.

What Excites Me in Tech

I'm fascinated by the intersection of data and decision-making. Automation, predictive modeling, and real-time dashboards are the tools I love working with because they bring instant value to stakeholders.

Tips for Aspiring Analysts

  • Master the basics of Excel, SQL, and Python
  • Focus on business impact, not just code
  • Communicate insights visually and clearly
